2021 - Some final thoughts

My thoughts on last moment of 2021
December 31, 2021 · 2 min · ngaongo.blog@gmail.com

Sitting here in front of my computer at the last moment of 2021, my mind is wandering around.

Leaving aside the crazy covid-19 epidemic, 2021 was a special year for me.

There were times when I was so frustrated, feeling empty and uncertain about the future to come.
There were moments of happiness that made tears come out of my eyes.
There were moments of joy that made me dance crazy like a child having a new toy.
There were times when I doubted myself.

Did I choose right? I think I did.

There is no doubt that 2022 will be marked as a special year in my life too.

I am going to be a father, very soon.
I am going to buy my first house with my hard-earned money.
What else? I don’t know. No one knows. Only those things are enough to make a single year become special.

Even now, at my 30s, I am still not sure about my passion. It’s not a big deal. Just trying to do my best, step by step, to become a better man is enough for now. When the time comes, it will come.

I want to live, not just survive!

“Planning where you want to go in life makes the difference”.

“Would you tell me, please, which way I ought to go from here?”
“That depends a good deal on where you want to get to,” said the Cat.
“I don’t much care where—” said Alice.
“Then it doesn’t matter which way you go,” said the Cat.
“—so long as I get somewhere,” Alice added as an explanation.
“Oh, you’re sure to do that,” said the Cat, “if you only walk long enough.”
